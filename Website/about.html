About Me

Hi I'm Jessie Ryce, a second year college student studying computer science. I currently attend Folsom Lake College, and I have plans to transfer to a university in the fall of 2019. I am a motivated student that takes interest in anything software-related. With each computer science course that I have taken in my college career, my enthusiasm for my future career has intensified. What drew me to pursue a degree in computer science is the fact that I can express my creativity through the use of technology to hopefully help people one day. While I was born and raised in a relatively small area, I believe that my mind belongs in a large city where I can contribute to the innovation that is happening every second.
